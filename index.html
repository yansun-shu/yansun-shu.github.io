About

Dr. Yan Sun is Shanghai Pujiang Talent. She received her Ph.D. from the University of Southampton, UK, in 2018, under the supervision of Professor Mark Nixon and Professor Jonathon Hare.

She has led and contributed to several national and provincial-level research projects, including a National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) project, the Scientific Research Fund for Outstanding Young Teachers (Key Project), and major NSFC projects and high-tech programs funded by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology.

Her research interests lie primarily in computer vision, deep learning, and video understanding, with a focus on the recognition and analysis of various motion patterns in videos.
