About

Dr. Yan Sun is an Assistant Professor in School of Computer Engineering and Science, Shanghai University, China. She obtained her Ph.D. degree from University of Southampton, UK in 2018, under the supervision of Professor Mark S. Nixon and Professor Jonathon S. Hare.

She received Shanghai Pujiang Program in 2020（上海市浦江人才计划项目获得者，2020年）. She has managed 1 National Natural Science Foundation Project in 2021（国家自然科学基金，2021年）, participated 1 National High-Tech Program and 1 MIIT Special Program for Ships as a key researcher .

Her research interests mainly focus on Computer Vision, Image Processing, analyzing different types of motion in videos, including gait analysis, action recognition, object tracking and etc.
